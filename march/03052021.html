5 MAR 2021

Who made your day happier/easier/more bearable? How did this person do that?

My blockmates made the morning classes bearable in their company, but that day saw two events that filled me with much excitement. Firstly, Bruno Mars made a triumphant return after almost five years, and he did so with a collaborative project with Anderson .Paak, an immensely talented artist whose cutting edge, warm soul music delights my senses.




SONG OF THE YEAR
The project, titled Silk Sonic, released this first single that day, and I soon as I pressed play on Leave The Door Open, I was floored. Itʼs as if a door had opened that would lead me to a world full of bliss. It is, by all accounts, a perfect song. Bruno & Anderson play their strengths well, and their voices blend so well. The chord progressions, melody, key changes, and adlibs turned my endocrine system into a constant stream of dopamine. I was left with much awe, rendering me speechless and making the long wait all worth it.

Meanwhile, later that evening, I found out that I had been accepted at UST Tiger Radio as a member of the Audio Production team. I was so happy when I learned the news, and it immediately made that day even more special to me.

What simple/major activity (depends on your definition of simple and major) did you have to do today that makes you feel happier/more rested/more refreshed?

Later that afternoon, we suddenly lost Internet connection and I wasnʼt able to surf the Internet to pass the time while waiting for a project meeting I was to attend in a few hours. It was there that I went to Logic Pro X to play around with a song I have on my computer Iʼve been thinking of sampling on an instrumental. I was inspired by Kanye West, arguably my favorite rapper and one of my favorite producers, and set out to make a beat that was a homage of his classic production style that involved speeding up samples. About an hour of tinkering and adding virtual instruments, I made the base idea of the beat, even adding demo vocals to fill in for future lyrics. It was a gratifying experience, and I was more than excited to come up with more ideas for songs.
