What simple/major activity (depends on your definition of simple and major) did you have to do today that makes you feel happier/more rested/more refreshed?

While this may sound mundane, the lip balm I bought on Monday arrived today, so I had to try it out. For years, I never bother to deal with my dry lips, but one day, they got so chapped that they cracked and bled. It was excruciatingly painful to move my lips around, and I finally got around to sorting this problem out. Putting it on and feeling how smooth my lips felt after put a smile on my face. This is self- care.
