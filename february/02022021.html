Who made your day happier/easier/more bearable? How did this person do that?

My blockmates, because they were great company during class as always. I love that we keep ourselves sane during discussion.
What school activity did you have to accomplish for today that you find difficult but fulfilling? Why is it fulfilling?

I started work on my contribution for a groupwork, which involved looking for certain examples of translation. I found it to be a fun, though difficult, task because I get to look into the content I enjoy to search for those.

What valuable lesson did you learn today that you would like to apply in your life moving forward?

I shouldnʼt be afraid to try new things. When the chance to improve my skills is present, I should seize it.
What simple/major activity did you have to do today that makes you feel happier/more rested/more refreshed?

After reading a slide for class, a blockmate joked about the way I said a certain sentence in it, which became a prompt for a script that I then recorded and sent to the groupchat for laughs. It helped me explore a kind of writing I donʼt usually do, and it opened up plenty of possibilities for future ideas.




What do you like about the kind of person that you have become today?

I liked that I had unlocked a new part of my writing abilities. I found a new way to express my ideas other than writing poems or long essays.
